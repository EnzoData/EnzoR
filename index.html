Who is EnzoData?

During my undergraduate studies, I was pursuing a career as an Actuary and that journey took me to St.Johns University, Purdue University and my final stop the Univeristy at Albany. Realizing that I wasn't happy pursuing that path, I decided to take a look at myself and asked the question of what I want in life. And then I embarked on a road towards Economics and I fell in love with Econometrics and data manipulation. Becoming a research assistant in Econometrics, it gave me the passion for data analytics through statistics and I knew from that moment on that I wanted to pursue a career in that spectrum. From that experience a Data Scientist has been born and I'm happy that I was given the opportunity to embark in the Masters program at the University at Albany.
